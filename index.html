ONSCREEN23

ONSCREEN23 is a project initiated by the Rotaract Club of IIT. Its main objective is to develop the cinematography skills of the upcoming and potential filmmakers, that will be increasing the number of practitioners in the field of cinematography.

The project consists of a series of workshops and a competition. By participating, the participants will be able to showcase their hidden talents with regards to cinematography, also to polish themselves when entering the competitive filming industry. ONSCREEN23 aspires to be a guiding hand for talented filmmakers as they develop their identities and brands as creative filmmakers.

Facts about ONSCREEN23

ONSCREEN23 is an open event that allows participants to showcase their cinematography skills in a competitive environment. Throughout this project, there will be a series of Workshops and virtual sessions, a Short Film Competition and Hands-on session, and, Final Competition.

Phase 01
Workshops & Sessions

A series of workshops and Sessions conducted by industry professionals aimed at improving the filmmaking skills of participants

Phase 02
Short Film Competition

A short film competition that participants can participate in as individuals or in teams. The short film is not limited to a theme

Phase 03
Screening Day and Awards Ceremony

The short movies of the finalists will be screened and the winners will be awarded with exclusive cash prizes.
